About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate VLSI Engineer with a strong focus on Design for Test (DFT). Currently, I work as a Senior Design Engineer at Marvell Semiconductors, where I'm part of the Data Center Engineering Business Unit. I specialize in end-to-end execution and in building robust test architectures and methodologies that ensure high-quality silicon and reliable performance.
